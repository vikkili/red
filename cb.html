CrunchBase Viz

UX Research on startup fundraising | Data visualization of CrunchBase.

The Challenge

Making Fundraising Easier for Startups

The project was initially a qualitative research on startup fundraising. Questions I wanted to explore include - What are the factors that will affect investment decision? What are the difficulties in startups' fundraising process? How to be fully prepared to get funding? At the end of research, I decided to design a visualization tool to leverage data for the use of investors as well as entrepreneurs.

Research

Interview, Observation & Focus Group (Yay!)

127 Surveys

  • Healthy eating and saving money are main motivations to cook more.
  • Dominant reason preventing them cooking more is "too busy to cook".

Sketches

Playing With Data

We brainestormed and sketched 48 ideas, which we later narrowed down to three by voting. The final winner was selected based on design requirements generated from our persona.

  • Quick: Time for prep and/or cooking is 30 minutes or less.
  • Simple: Limited number of ingredients and few or no hard-to-find ingredients.
  • Affordable: Little to no added cost.
  • Customizable: Offers a variety of food options based on user preference.
  • Convenient: No significant, unique time management required

Build

Crunchbase Viz

Customized Recipe Recommendation

Recipes recommended everyday are based on preferred cooking time, servings and dietary requirements customized in settings.

Simple and Quick Preparation

You can add all ingredients to cart with only one click, or only select needed ingredients. Simple instructions with audio play are there to help novice chefs.

Online Shopping & In-store Pickup

The amount of ingredients added into cart can be adjusted and other ingredients can be searched and added. Choose a nearest store and you can conveniently pick up your items by drive-through with no delivery costs.
