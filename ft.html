FTime

Mobile app design to help people keep in touch with family & friends.

The Challenge

How can we help families keep in touch on a regular basis?

This is a research group project, where we started out on how to leverage technology on family relationship. After informative interviews with 10 participants, we found it a common pain point to contact families on a regular basis, due to busy schedule or time difference, etc. On the one hand, parents want to know what's going on with their kids and don't know when is the best time to call. On the other hand, children feel obliged to call parents but often forget. So how can we make this two-way communication eaiser?

Concept Testing & Iteration

1st Round

Features:

  • Track how long it's been since your last call.
  • Automatically generating 5 recent photoes to share.
  • Display contacts' availability on homepage

Main Feedbacks:

  • "Traking is useful. Can it be accurate? Do I need to adjust manually?"
  • "I use Whatsapp to share photoes with my parents. Why would I use this?"

2nd Round

Iteration:

  • Eliminated photo sharing.
  • Shifted focus to reminder/notification. Prompt you when you and your contacts are available - "Your mom is available now. Wanna call her?"
  • Manually input availability or sync with calendar.

Main Feedbacks:

  • "I don't think either calendar or input can reflect my actural availability to talk to them. It's too flexible."
  • "It's annoying if it sends me a notification everytime I'm free."

3rd Round

Iteration:

  • Surveyed on "free time" and availability with 49 participants. Frequent answers are "while driving", "on bus" or "at home after dinner".
  • Allow users to show availability with the combination of time, location detect and mobility, e.g.: at home after 9pm.
  • Allow user to set goals. Only send notification when it's too long since they call their contacts.

Main Feedbacks:

  • "I want to be able to see their time zone too."
  • "The length of conversation is as important as frequency."
  • "The reminder makes me feel guilty, but it's effective."

Final Solution

FTime Prototype

Track and Show Availability

You can check how long you talked to them over the past weeks. You can also get a quick glance of everyone's availability and directly contact them via in-app link.

Flexible Availability Setting

You can set your availability either by time, location or a combination of them. You can even have the "on commute" option.

Setting Goals Without Pressure

We are not making you nervous or feeling guilty about not calling them, we have little heart as indicators always there to represent your love.
