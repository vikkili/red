FTime

Mobile app design to help people keep in touch with family & friends.

The Challenge

How can we help family and friends keep in touch on a regular basis?

As international students, we clearly see our parents' painpoint when they face the limitation of contacting us, due to our busy schedule or different time zone. We ourselves also feel obliged to contact them, however we often forget. So how can we make this two-way communication easier? After research, we found it a common painpoint existing not only among international students and their parents, but also families who live in different cities.

Testing & Iteration

4 Rounds of Iteration

We used paper prototype and sketches to run usability test and went through 4 quick rounds of iteration. Changes were made mainly based on user feedbacks. As we planned to built this app out with real code, feasibility is also taken into consideration.

  • Tracking: Tracking the calling history in other apps like Skype might not be feasible, so we track only iOS native phone call, facetime and message.
  • Availability: Syncing from calendar doesn't accurately reflects availability.
  • Reminder: Users don't need availability notifications, so we only notify them about goals.
  • Photo Sharing: Users already use other tools to share photoes, so we eliminated the feature.
  • Goal Setting: Provide option to notify them when they haven't complete their goals, add gamification badges.

Final Solution

FTime Prototype

Track and Show Availability

You can check how long you talked to them over the past weeks. You can also get a quick glance of everyone's availability and directly contact them via in-app link.

Flexible Availability Setting

You can set your availability either by time, location or a combination of them. You can even have the "on commute" option.

Setting Goals Without Pressure

We are not making you nervous or feeling guilty about not calling them, we have little heart as indicators always there to represent your love.
