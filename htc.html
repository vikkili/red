HTC RE CAMERA

HTC Sponsored Usability Study of RE Camera and the iOS App.

The Challenge

How to Improve the User Experience of RE?

The HTC RE is a small, screen-less, waterproof camera that connects to smartphones via Bluetooth and WiFi. The RE app allows users to see the viewfinder, browse the files stored on the camera, download or share those files, and modify the camera's settings. HTC is now building a second version of RE Camera and wants usability study of their existing version to improve the user experience. Our goal is to improve the usability of the camera and application from a different and unbiased perspective.

Planning

Paticipants & Tasks

Participants

  • Male and female, 20 ~ 35 years old
  • Proficient with iPhone
  • Open to new IT product
  • Take photos with iPhone frequently
  • Preferably a parent or pet owner

Scenarios

  • You see your puppy outside and want to record it using RE. You already understand the buttons and functions of RE camera and have set up your RE camera connected to the RE App.
  • Now you are at work without RE camera, and want to show the pictures to your co-workers.

Tasks

  • Take 5 pictures using RE and app
  • Take 5 pictures using only RE
  • Take 5 ten to thirty second slo-motion
  • Change the time-lapse setting
  • Take pictures & videos with iPhone
  • Review photos and videos in the gallery and compare
  • Show pictures without RE around

Positive Feedbacks

Original hardware design.

5 out of 7 participants expressed RE is a good size and easy to hold.

It's small, lightweight, easy to carry around and fit in my small purse. Looks cool and original.
It has big shutter button. I like pressing a real button. I heard a sound, so I guess it started shooting.

RE has cool video features and is useful in many scenarios.

5 out of 7 participants mentioned it could be useful in scenarios like road trip, sports, hiking, concerts and rainy days.

The time lapse is pretty cool. I don't need to sit next to it all the time.
I could use it in the concert while being in the moment, no screen in front of your face.

Opportunities for Improvement

Can't review gallery when RE is away or disconnected.

I was expecting it to do something, but it doesn't do anything. Apparently, it disconnects when I can't find RE. Not clear that you can't see the photos. It's really crazy that it does that.

SUGGESTION: Show RE photos and videos as a part of the global library.

Icons are not recognizable

I see two icons here, this one is video, I don't know what the walking man is.

SUGGESTION: Change the icons according to iOS standards, and make them distinctive.

Button and light are not intuitive

I donâ€™t know how long to hold the button to take a video.
I didn't notice there was a light that gives feedback.

SUGGESTION: Remove activation for slow-mo, start recording using the front button.Use brighter lights.

RE can't focus or zoom in and out.

With iPhone you can tap and get focus to blur the background. But now I tap the screen, and it doesn't respond.

IPhone photo quality is better than RE.

I'd rather use an iPhone. If RE wants to be seen as a competitor to the Go Pro, the quality needs to be improved.

RE app is delayed and laggy and RE battery doesn't last long.

This thing freezes a lot. It's very laggy.
