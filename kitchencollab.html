KITCHEN COLLAB

Mobile app design that helps busy people to cook more often.

The Challenge

How can we help people to cook more often?

Cooking at home is a great way to spend time with loved ones. It also empowers people to take control of their nutrition and decide each ingredient that goes into a meal. Yet, even with all of these benefits, many people still don't cook at home as often as they may like, for they think cooking as difficult, time-consuming or dull.

Research

Methods & Findings

127 Surveys

  • Healthy eating and saving money are main motivations to cook more.
  • Dominant reason preventing them cooking more is "too busy to cook".

9 Interviews & 170 Notes

  • Having hands-on ingredients is necessary for cooking.
  • Easy and fast are the most desirable features to support cooking more.
  • Planning ahead is helpful.

20 Competitive Product Analysis

  • Existing products or services are either too expensive, time consuming to navigate or not customiable.
  • Most products are focusing on recipes.

Persona

Helen and Bud

Helen - Healthy Wife

Goal: Quickly cook healthy meals for family

" Call me a foodie if you want. My family deserves good-quality healthy meals. "

Bud - Budget Student

Goal: Save money by cooking at home

" I really need to get serious about my budget. Eating out costs a lot. "

Ideation

Champion of 48 Ideas

We brainestormed and sketched 48 ideas, which we later narrowed down to three by voting. The final winner was selected based on design requirements generated from our persona.

  • Quick: Time for prep and/or cooking is 30 minutes or less.
  • Simple: Limited number of ingredients and few or no hard-to-find ingredients.
  • Affordable: Little to no added cost.
  • Customizable: Offers a variety of food options based on user preference.
  • Convenient: No significant, unique time management required

Prototype

5 Rounds of Iteration

We used Sketch to mockup the UI and used Flinto to make it into interactive prototype for usability testing. After 5 rounds of iteration, I still find the following issues to be improved.

  • Tab navigation should be replaced with side bar.
  • More detail should be added to reflect the design requirements.
  • Visual design should follow iOS standard.

Based on the logic of prototype and a moodboard design, I redesigned a final solution for the design problem.

Final Solution

Kitchen Collab Prototype

Customized Recipe Recommendation

Recipes recommended everyday are based on preferred cooking time, servings and dietary requirements customized in settings.

Simple and Quick Preparation

You can add all ingredients to cart with only one click, or only select needed ingredients. Simple instructions with audio play are there to help novice chefs.

Online Shopping & In-store Pickup

The amount of ingredients added into cart can be adjusted and other ingredients can be searched and added. Choose a nearest store and you can conveniently pick up your items by drive-through with no delivery costs.
