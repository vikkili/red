PRIMAL ATHLETICS

Website redesign for a crossfit gym.

The Challenge

How can the website better serve client's business goal?

Primal Athletics is a crossfit gym with growing business. Their current website can't serve their business goal properly. Our client hopes to achieve following goals with our redesign:

  • Improve Usability: Allow users to effeciently complete tasks and find information.
  • Consistent Branding: Keep the original logo and match branding to that.
  • Emphasize Gym Gulture: Better represent their mission and culture.
  • Business Growth: Attract new members and encourage current members to signup

Persona

Meet Carrie and Nick

Carrie - Current

Needs: Quickly locate WOD and class schedule; easily register for class.

" I want to feel like my gym is my home. "

Nick - Newbie

Needs: Get a feel for gym culture and find pricing information; knowledge on crossfit.

" Wait, what is crossfit? "

Information Architecture

Navigate the Site

We extracted 62 content pieces from the website and conducted open category card sort with 5 participants. We then used the result to direct our IA. Almost all of our participants agreed on the top level navigation.

Prototype & Iteration

Sketches, Wireframes & Hi-fi

We started from sketches and wireframes. After three rounds of iteration based on usability tests, we produced high-fidelity prototype. Key changes after iteration include:

  • Revisited content hierarchy to reduce heavy texts in wireframe.
  • Eliminated second level navigation in sub-pages to avoid confusion.
  • Added more visual elements to call out important information and actions.

However, due to limited time, there are still problems with the hi-fi prototype:

  • Still text heavy, visual design is out-dated.
  • Information redundancy between top nav landing page and 2nd level nav.
  • Frequently visited pages are called out on the not prominent up right corner.
